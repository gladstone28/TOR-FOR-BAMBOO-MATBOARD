Background

Jamaica's rich bamboo resources offer an excellent opportunity for sustainable industrial development. The Bamboo Matboard Production Facility aims to leverage local bamboo resources to produce high-quality matboards, contributing to economic development and environmental sustainability.

Objectives

  • Conduct a comprehensive study to assess the viability of bamboo matboard production in Jamaica.
  • Develop local expertise in bamboo cultivation and matboard production.
  • Establish a fully operational bamboo matboard production facility.
  • Develop distribution channels both within and outside Jamaica.
