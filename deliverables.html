List of Project Deliverables

Here is a list of key deliverables that the Bamboo Matboard Production Facility Project aims to achieve:

  • Feasibility Report: A comprehensive document detailing the findings of the feasibility study, including recommendations for the project’s continuation.
  • Training Program Materials: Manuals and training guides developed for the training programs.
  • Production Facility: A fully operational production facility capable of producing high-quality bamboo matboards.
  • Marketing Plan: A strategic document outlining the marketing and sales strategies for the bamboo matboards.
